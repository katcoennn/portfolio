About Us

10% of teens who drive have done so with alcohol in their system.

We are 4 high school students from New York City and New Jersey who felt passionate about putting an end to drunk driving. SteerClear is our attempt to alleviate the problems surrounding parties- specifically, how to get home when one is legally impaired. Through the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Goldman Sachs, we came together and learned how to code and developed this app.
